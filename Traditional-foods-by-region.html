Traditional Foods By Region

1. North Africa

Influenced by Arab, Berber, and Mediterranean cuisines

North Africa Food
Country Example Traditional Food
Morocco Couscous, Tagine, Harira soup, Pastilla
Egypt Koshari, Ful medames, Molokhia, Taameya (Egyptian falafel)
Algeria Chakhchoukha, Rechta, Mechoui
Tunisia Brik, Ojja, Lablabi (chickpea soup)
Libya Bazin, Asida, Couscous with lamb or chicken

2. West Africa

Rich in spices, palm oil, and starchy foods (yam, cassava)

West African Foods
Country Examples Traditional Foods
Nigeria Jollof rice, Egusi soup, Pounded yam, Moi Moi, Suya
Ghana Waakye, Banku and tilapia, Fufu with light soup, Kelewele
Senegal Thieboudienne (jollof fish), Yassa chicken, Mafé (peanut stew)
Ivory Coast Attiéké, Kedjenou, Alloco (fried plantains)
Mali Tô (millet dough), Sauce gombo (okra sauce), Djouka

3. East Africa

Blends of Bantu, Nilotic, Indian, and Arab influences

East African Foods
Country Examples Traditional Foods
Kenya Ugali, Sukuma wiki, Nyama choma, Githeri, Chapati
Ethiopia Injera with Doro Wat, Tibs, Shiro, Kitfo
Tanzania Ugali, Ndizi nyama (banana and meat stew), Wali wa nazi (coconut rice)
Somalia Bariis iskukaris (spiced rice), Muufo, Canjeero, Suqaar
Uganda Matoke (steamed bananas), Luwombo, Posho, Rolex (chapati and eggs)

4. Central Africa

Heavy use of cassava, fish, and spicy stews

Central African Foods
Country Examples Traditional Foods
DR Congo Moambe chicken, Fufu, Saka-saka (cassava leaves), Pondu
Cameroon Ndolé (bitterleaf stew), Koki (black-eyed pea pudding), Achu soup
Gabon Poulet Nyembwe (palm nut chicken), Cassava fufu
Chad Jarret de boeuf, Kisra (fermented bread), Daraba okra stew
Central African Republic Gozo (cassava), Kanda (meatballs), Peanut-based sauces

5. Southern Africa

Maize-based staples, barbecues, and indigenous greens

Southern African Foods
Country Examples Traditional Foods
South Africa Pap (maize porridge), Chakalaka, Boerewors, Bunny chow, Biltong
Zimbabwe Sadza, Muriwo (leafy greens), Nyama, Peanut butter stew
Zambia Nshima, Ifisashi (vegetables in peanut sauce), Chikanda (African polony)
Namibia Kapana (street meat), Oshifima, Mopane worms
Botswana Seswaa (shredded meat), Bogobe, Morogo (wild spinach)
